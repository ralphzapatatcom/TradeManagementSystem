User Manual: Trade Management System
1. Introduction
The Trade Management System is an all-in-one tool designed for commercial management. It allows for controlling product flow, managing clients, recording detailed sales, and supervising daily cash reconciliation.

2. Login
The system uses role-based access control to ensure security.

Available Roles:

Administrator: Full access to all features, including user management and reports.

Salesperson: Limited access to performing sales and basic inventory management.

3. System Modules
3.1. Inventory Management
This module allows you to maintain precise control over merchandise.

Products: Registration of items using unique barcodes, purchase/sale prices, and stock control.

Categories: Organization of products to facilitate searches and reporting.

Merchandise Entries: Registration of new acquisitions linked to specific suppliers.

3.2. Supplier Management
Allows for storing detailed information about suppliers, including company name, contact, and phone number, facilitating communication for stock replenishment.

3.3. Sales Module
This is the operational core of the system.

Sales Recording: Allows generating transactions linked to a client, a user (salesperson), and a specific payment method.

Sales Details: Automated system that records which products were sold, in what quantity, and at what unit price for each transaction.

Clients: Database to register contact information and IDs/tax numbers of recurring clients.

3.4. Administration and Cash
Payment Methods: Management of the ways a client pays (e.g., cash, credit card, bank transfer).

Cash Reconciliation: Daily recording of initial and final cash amounts, ensuring transparency in cash flow when closing a shift.

4. Common Workflows
How to perform a sale?
Identify or register the Client in the corresponding module.

Add the desired Products to the order.

Select the Payment Method.

Confirm the sale; the system will automatically reduce the product stock and save the operation details.

How to register new merchandise?
Select the product in the catalog.

Go to the Merchandise Entries section.

Indicate the supplier and the quantity received.

The system will update the stock_actual field in the products table.

5. Support and Security
Data Integrity: The system uses robust database engines to ensure that when a client or supplier is deleted, historical sales are not lost but are handled correctly through referential integrity rules.

Security: User passwords are encrypted, and access is restricted by roles.
